# COMPETITION VOLUNTEERS

If you would like to volunteer at the largest competition to be held in South America, this is your opportunity. You will need to answer a few questions.

**Remember!**

If you are selected as a competition volunteer, you will receive several benefits. These will be communicated to the email address you use to register once your application has been approved by the organizing team and the **SAC2026** delegates.
