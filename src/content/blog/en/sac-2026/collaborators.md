# Collaborators

SAC 2026 is supported by collaborators who contribute their talent and passion for speedcubing to make this championship a unique experience.

---

## Legión Cubo

<div style="display: flex; justify-content: center; margin: 1.5em 0;">
 <img src="/sac/legion_cubo.png" style="width: clamp(120px, 25vw, 220px); height: auto; border-radius: 12px;" alt="Legión Cubo">
</div>

**Legión Cubo** is a project created by **Alexander Montoya** with the goal of driving the growth of speedcubing through technology. It specializes in the development of websites, applications, and platforms related to the community, as well as the creation of content and videos about apps, tools, and innovative projects for speedcubers.
