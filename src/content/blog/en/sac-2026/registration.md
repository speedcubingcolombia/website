## How to Register

Registration is done exclusively through the official WCA website:

1. **Create your WCA account** (if you don't have one yet) at [worldcubeassociation.org](https://www.worldcubeassociation.org/)
2. Search for **"SAC 2026"** or **"South American Championship 2026"** in the competition calendar
3. Click on **"Register"**
4. Select the events you wish to compete in
5. Complete the payment following the instructions
6. Wait for confirmation from the organizing team

## Registration Fees

### Competitors

- **Early bird** (until March 31): $50 USD
- **Regular registration** (April 1 - April 30): $60 USD  
- **Late registration** (May 1 - May 31): $70 USD

Registration includes:

- Participation in all selected events
- Official SAC 2026 t-shirt
- Competitor badge
- Welcome kit

### Spectators

- **General admission:** $10 USD per day
- **Full pass (4 days):** $30 USD

Spectator tickets will be available on the event website soon.

## Competitor Limit

**Maximum capacity:** 350 competitors

Registration works on a first-come, first-served basis. Once the limit is reached, a waiting list will open. We recommend registering as soon as possible to secure your spot.

## Refund Policy

## Requirements and Regulations

### Qualification Times

Some events require qualification times registered in the WCA:

- **6x6x6:** 4:30.00 or better
- **7x7x7:** 7:00.00 or better
- **3x3x3 Fewest Moves:** Average of 50 moves or better

Check the official event page for the complete list of requirements.

### Time Limits

Each event will have specific time limits. If you don't complete your solve within the limit, it will be recorded as DNF (Did Not Finish).

### Competition Format

Most events will use the **Average of 5** format (average of 5 attempts). Some events will use:

- **Best of 3:** 6x6x6, 7x7x7
- **Best of 2:** FMC, MBLD
- **Mean of 3:** 4x4x4 BLD, 5x5x5 BLD

## Additional Information

### WCA Account

If this is your first WCA competition, the system will automatically create your WCA ID after the event. This ID will accompany you throughout your speedcubing career and record all your records.

### Changes After Registration

You can modify the events you participate in until **June 5, 2026** at no additional cost. After this date, no changes will be allowed.

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I register on the day of the event?**  
No. All registration must be done online in advance.

**Do I need to be a South American citizen to compete?**  
No. SAC 2026 is open to competitors from around the world, although priority is given to South American competitors if capacity is reached.

**What happens if the list is full?**  
A waiting list will open. If someone cancels, we will contact you by email.
