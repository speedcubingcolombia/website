## How to register

Registration is carried out exclusively through the official WCA website:

1. **Create your WCA account** (if you do not already have one) at [worldcubeassociation.org](https://www.worldcubeassociation.org/)
2. Search for [**"SAC 2026"**](https://www.worldcubeassociation.org/competitions/SAC2026) or **"South American Championship 2026"** in the competition calendar
3. Click on **"Register"**
4. Select the events you wish to compete in
5. Complete the payment according to the instructions
6. Wait for confirmation from the organizing team

## Registration fees

### Competitors

- **First date** (until March 18): $80.000 COP
- **Second date** (March 19 - April 19): $100.000 COP  
- **Third date** (April 20 - May 28): $120.000 COP

Registration includes:

- Participation in all selected events
- Official SAC 2026 souvenir
- Competitor badge
- WCA SAC 2026 apparel

### Spectators

- **General admission:** $5.000 COP per day
- **Full pass (4 days):** $20.000 COP

Payment will be made upon entry to the competition venue

## Competitor limit

**Maximum capacity:** 400 competitors

Registration works on a first-come, first-served basis. Once the limit is reached, a waiting list will be opened. **We recommend registering as early as possible to secure your spot.**

## Requirements and regulations

### Qualification times

Some events require qualification times registered in the WCA:

- **4x4x4 Blindfolded** Better than 10:00.00 before May 28, 2026.
- **5x5x5 Blindfolded** Better than 20:00.00 before May 28, 2026.
- **Multi - Blindfolded** Better than 12 points before May 28, 2026.

Check the official event page for the complete list of requirements.

### Time limits

Each event will have specific time limits. If you do not complete your solve within the limit, it will be recorded as a DNF (Did Not Finish).

### Competition format

Most events will use the **Average of 5** format. Some events will use:

- **Mean of 3:** 6x6x6, 7x7x7
- **Best of 2:** MBLD
- **Best of 3:** 4x4x4 BLD, 5x5x5 BLD

## Additional information

### WCA account

If this is your first WCA competition, the system will automatically create your WCA ID after the event. This ID will accompany you throughout your speedcubing career and record all your results.

### Changes after registration

You may modify the events you will participate in until **May 28, 2026** at no additional cost. After this date, no changes will be allowed.

### Frequently asked questions

**Can I register on the day of the event?**  
No. All registration must be completed online in advance.

**Do I need to be a South American citizen to compete?**  
Anyone from anywhere in the world can participate in the competition, regardless of nationality.

**What happens if the list is full?**  
A waiting list will be opened. If someone cancels, we will contact you by email.
