### Other Events

## Important Information

### Arrival

- Arrive **at least 30 minutes before** your first event
- Bring your **ID**
- **Mandatory check-in** each day

### Groups

- You will be assigned to specific groups
- Check the **schedule on the WCA page**
- Groups may change, check constantly

### Roles

As a competitor, you will participate in:

- **Competing:** Solving your cubes
- **Judging:** Judging other competitors
- **Scrambling:** Mixing cubes
- **Running:** Transporting cubes

### Last-minute Changes

The schedule may be adjusted based on:

- Final number of competitors
- Round progress
- Unforeseen situations

- Venue announcements
- Official app notifications
- Results screens
